personal website

Traveller, Enthusiast, Moony

 
I am a Human Behaviour and Cognition Researcher interested in Vision, HCI and a lot more. I graduated from the University of Padova with a thesis on Scene Perception and Situation Awareness in Novice and Experienced Drivers. After graduation I moved to the Schepens Eye Research Institute in Boston where I worked with Dr. Alex Bowers researching on Peripheral Vision. After a few proficuous years, I started my current job at Northeastern University as an independently funded fellow in Dr. Peter Bex in the Translational Vision Lab.
